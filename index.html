I am currently a postdoctoral fellow in the Department of Mathematics at the Hong Kong University of Science and Technology, working with Prof. Jian-Feng Cai. I received my Ph.D. degree from the Department of Electronic and Computer Engineering at the same university in 2022, advised by Prof. Daniel P. Palomar.

My research interests are mainly on the intersection of machine learning, signal processing, numerical optimization, data science, and high-dimensional statistics.
